Who we are

ISAC is a service intended to help HSL IFAR faculty and staff plan and conduct all aspects of your clinical trial or intervention study. After an initial consultation, we will prepare a report summarizing key considerations and recommendations specific to your investigation.

How to obtain an ISAC consultation

The first step is to complete a brief request form available on the ISAC website. The ISAC Program Director, Elaine Bergman, will review your request, and set up an initial consultation with the appropriate ISAC team members.

The request asks general questions including:

1. The overall goals and stage of development of your study;
2. Basic design questions such as plans for enrollment and number of participant visits;
3. Funding sources;
4. Aspects of the project for which help is needed (e.g., statistical design, regulatory issues, or project management);
5. Project timelines.
